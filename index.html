Explainable Artificial Intelligence Workshop

Workshop Description

This workshop focuses on Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) for healthcare systems, bridging the gap between sophisticated algorithmic models and the clinical necessity for trust, fairness, and robustness.

Guided by industry experts, participants will traverse the landscape of global and local XAI, applying these frameworks to complex medical imaging and tabular healthcare data.
