Interpretable and Transparent AI for Healthcare Applications

Explainable Artificial Intelligence Workshop

Date: 10 February 2026

Workshop Description

This workshop focuses on Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) for healthcare systems, aiming to bridge the gap between advanced AI models and their reliable, trustworthy deployment in medical applications. As AI-driven solutions increasingly support clinical decision-making, explainability, transparency, and interpretability are essential to ensure trust, fairness, and robustness.

Participants will explore both global and local XAI approaches, with applications to tabular healthcare data (e.g., electronic health records and clinical variables) and medical imaging data. Through lectures and hands-on sessions, they will learn to apply interpretable and transparent AI methods to real-world healthcare systems.
