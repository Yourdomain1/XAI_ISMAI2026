Trustworthy AI in Healthcare: Transparency, Interpretability, and Ethics

Explainable Artificial Intelligence Workshop

Date: 10 February 2026

Workshop Description

This workshop focuses on Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) for healthcare systems, aiming to bridge the gap between advanced AI models and their reliable, trustworthy use in medical applications. As AI-driven solutions increasingly support clinical decision-making, explainability becomes essential to ensure transparency, fairness, robustness, and user trust.

The workshop introduces both global and local XAI approaches, with a particular emphasis on interpreting models applied to tabular healthcare data (such as electronic health records and clinical variables) and medical imaging data. Through a combination of theoretical presentations and hands-on sessions, participants will explore state-of-the-art XAI techniques and real-world healthcare use cases.
